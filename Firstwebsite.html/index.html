Why I Joined the Front End Engineering Program

I joined the Front End Engineering program at ALT School to gain the skills needed to design and develop applications that are both interactive and impactful. As a teacher, I often felt limited in bringing to life the dynamic, engaging learning experiences I envisioned for my students. This program provides a structured path, guided by experienced developers and supported by a collaborative community of learners—an environment where I can grow and thrive.

ALT School’s focus on practical, real-world skills makes it an ideal place for me to deepen my knowledge of modern technologies like React and to build responsive, user-friendly interfaces that can transform how people experience education.
